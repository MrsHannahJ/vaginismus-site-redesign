What is Vaginismus?

Symptoms

Depending on the intensity, vaginismus symptoms range from minor burning sensations with tightness to total closure of the vaginal opening with impossible penetration.

Learn More

Causes

Vaginismus is a perplexing condition because its cause may be difficult to pinpoint. It may result from a combination of physical, emotional, or relational issues.

Learn More

Diagnosis

Women often suspect they have vaginismus from their symptoms. Medical diagnosis is typically determined by gynecological exam to rule out the possibility of other conditions.

Learn More

Treatment

Vaginismus is considered one of the most successfully treatable female sexual disorders. Studies have shown treatment success rates approaching nearly 100%.

Did you know that diminished estrogen levels due to age, childbirth, cancer treatments, or surgery may result in vaginal atrophy? Vaginismus can affect women of all ages, even if they have experience with pain-free intercourse.
